Separate models/tables for tile types, with shared tile functionality included in a module

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mahjong_tiles

TABLES          MODELS        INCLUDE
circle_tiles    CircleTile    suit and rank stuff
bamboo_tiles    BambooTile    suit and rank stuff
character_tiles CharacterTile suit and rank stuff
wind_tiles      WindTile      honor tile stuff
dragon_tiles    DragonTile    honor tile stuff
flower_tiles    FlowerTile    flower tile stuff (overall group, not particular flowery tiles)
season_tiles    SeasonTile    flower tile stuff (overall group, not particular flowery tiles)
